Natural Disasters Analysis

Final Project | Tableau Dashboard | Data Analysis

Project Overview

This project analyzes global natural disasters between 1970–2021. The dashboard explores disaster trends, affected populations, economic damages, regional differences and major disaster types around the world.

Tools Used

Tableau | Excel | Data Cleaning | Data Visualization | Dashboard Design

Main Research Question

How have natural disasters changed over time, and which regions and disaster types have had the strongest impact on populations and economic damages?

Key Insights

  • Natural disasters show different patterns across regions and years.
  • Some disaster types create higher economic damages, while others affect larger populations.
  • Regional exposure plays a major role in the severity and frequency of disasters.
